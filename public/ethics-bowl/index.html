CSM 2022 Ethics Bowl

What is the Ethics Bowl?

"An ethics bowl is a competitive yet collaborative event in which students discuss real life ethical issues. In each round of competition, teams take turns analyzing ethical cases and responding to questions and comments from the other team and a panel of judges. [...]
NHSEB goal is to do more than teach students how to think through ethical issues: It is to teach students how to think through ethical issues together, as fellow citizens in a complex moral and political community." [Source]
